About Us

The Toronto Sock Exchange seeks to provide warm socks and other living necessities to those in need. We achieve this ambition through the sale of fancy dress socks within large corporations, using 100% of our profits to purchase warm items to donate.

Success

In our pilot year at 1 company we were able to sell over 600 pairs of fancy socks, donating 2400 warm wool socks to homeless shelters across the GTA. This year, we have increased our network to 10 companies, and hope to see a commensurate increase in profits & donations.
